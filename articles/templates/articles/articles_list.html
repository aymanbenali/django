Composite carbon chassis

Hercules’s chassis is a composite moncoque chassis which is integrated into the composite lower body. Designed to protect the driver from impacts, it serves as the backbone of the car.

Electrical system

The high voltage system includes the battery pack, embedded charging unit, motor controller and motor, A CAN network links the primary vehicle computer to various monitoring components in order to alert the driver of any irregularities, and enables constant communication between the car and the support vehicles.

Autonomous System

Hercules’s has two cameras that acquire in real time the landscape in front of it, and uses those images in the autonomous driving mode

Aerodynamic body

Hercules has an aerodynamic shape that reduces the consumption of energy.
